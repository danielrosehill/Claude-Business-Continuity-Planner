# Claude Business Continuity Planner

A Claude Code workspace for developing Business Continuity Management (BCM) programs aligned with ISO 22301 and industry best practices.

## Quick Start

Open in Claude Code and run `/init` to initialize the workspace.

## Commands

- `/init` - Initialize workspace, capture organization profile
- `/bia` - Business Impact Analysis
- `/risk` - Risk Assessment
- `/bcplan` - Business Continuity Plan
- `/irp [type]` - Incident Response Plan (cyber, facility, supplier, etc.)
- `/crisis` - Crisis Management Plan

## What It Does

- Guides you through organization intake
- Generates BIA, risk assessments, and BC plans
- Creates incident response and crisis management documentation
- Outputs follow BCM standards with proper document control

---

Based on the [Claude Preparedness Planner](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Claude-Preparedness-Planner) concept, adapted for formal business continuity management.
